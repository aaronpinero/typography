About Tyfy

Tyfy (short for typography or typogrify) started as – and to some degree, remains – a personal project, an inventory of previous work, and resumé filler. It is inspired by the writings of Robert Bringhurst and the work of designers like Matej Latin and Richard Rutter.

I also have a related project, Tyfy Bootstrap, that applies elements of this framework as a theme for Bootstrap.
